Bigg Boss is a popular reality television show that originated in the Netherlands and has been adapted in various countries, including India. The Indian version of Bigg Boss follows the format of a competitive reality show where a group of celebrity contestants live together in a specially constructed house for a fixed duration of time, usually around three months.
